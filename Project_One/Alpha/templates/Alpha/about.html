How this website came to be

This website was initially meant to be a minor project as a means of practicing coding but whilst i was making this site, i was also playing TES: Skyrim and

I wanted to read the books that you can find scattered across the large land of Skyrim

But I found it profoundly annoying to look for every single book in the game and even if i did find every single book, it would still be scattered around the various houses, chests, and etc.

And i also wanted to easily find what i wanted to read, a series of book in the same page

And so i have looked for every book in Skyrim and typed them word by word for this website

And i will be updating other books from TESO and TES: Oblivion
